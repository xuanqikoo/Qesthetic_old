ABOUT ME

Introduction

Who Am I?

My name is Koo Xuan Qi, I am 19 years old and currently pursuing a Diploma in Multimedia Design. I have been immersed in the world of design for 14 years. Through this platform, I aim to inspire you to delve into the creative industry, showcasing my best work and encouraging you to do the same.

Experiences

What have I done?

I started learning traditional art at the age of 5 at an art studio called 'Makers' in my hometown, Ipoh. When I was 16,I began exploring digital art by learning Photoshop and Illustrator. At 18, I purchased my first APS-C camera and started learning photography and videography. Now, as a university student, I provide tuition to my classmates and work as a freelance designer in my free time.
